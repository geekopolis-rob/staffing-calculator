About Ratios

Staff-to-child ratios are required by licensing regulations and vary by age group.

Format: Enter ratios as "staff:children" (e.g., "1:4" means 1 staff member per 4 children).

Common Ratios
  • Infants (0-12 mo): 1:4
  • Toddlers (12-24 mo): 1:6
  • Preschool (2-3 yr): 1:8
  • Preschool (3-5 yr): 1:12

Note: These are examples. Check your state/local regulations for specific requirements.
